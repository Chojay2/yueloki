KUZUZANGPO LA WELCOME TO HOTEL YUE LO KI

Thank you for choosing to stay in our Hotel and allowing us to show our culture of authentic and seamless Bhutanese service. Hotel Yue Lo Ki is located only a few minutes from the incredible Phobjikha valley. The panoramic viewed valley is home to magnificent black- necked cranes and astonishing flora and fauna. The valley also has a lot of beautiful nature trails to hike and trek. The valley has numerous religious and cultural sites of immense significance to the people of Bhutan. A truly unique experience of wellness, spirituality, and nature in one place.

We have 16 rooms which consist of 11 twin beds and four king-size beds. All the rooms have rich and elegant wooden panels that provide excellent acoustics insulation, great for the cold Phobjikha valley. The walls of windows in the rooms offer a fantastic panoramic view of the valley and a centralized water heating system for a relaxing shower after your adventures.
