Phobjikha (Bhutan)

A place worth visiting with astonishing valleys and mountains. Phobkikha, which falls under Wangduephodrang district, is a place where most of the people from here and outside the country wishes to visit. The rich culture that the place has to offer is one of the main reasons people visit. It is also the place where one of the rarest bird (Black Necked Crane) is found mostly during the fall.
