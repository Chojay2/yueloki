Our Experiences

Picnic and hoisting prayer flags

Experience one of the most relaxing and enjoyable picnic spots of Phobjikha valley. Take a mini hike down the valley to Damchen Lhakhang, a 14th-century monastery, and immerse in the lifestyles of local monks. After that, walk towards the local prayer flags hoisting hill and unfold delicious packed lunch at an elevation of 3800 meters accompanied by scenic views of the valley and, if in luck, the sight of the majestic Black-necked-Cranes.

Prayer flags are beautiful colours of scripted texts and an essential part of the Bhutanese tradition. After Lunch, Prayer flags will be hoisted to share and disseminate prosperity and luck to all sentient beings.

Nature trek

Phobjikha valley has plenty of treks depending on your fitness level. These hiking trails are very ancient and used by ancestors to travel between villages before the advent of the road. Hikers can indulge their senses with the luscious meadows, crystal clear streams, beautiful flora, and magnificent birds. The hiking trails will let you experience the authentic traditional Bhutanese way of living and unique architecture.

Mountain Biking

Phobjikha valley is the perfect place to mountain bike, with less car traffic and naturally paved trails that intercepts beautiful villages, monasteries, and the most beautiful landscapes. Our friendly team at the reception can help you book a mountain bike and a guide if required.

Pema Lingpa is the most revered and celebrated saint of Bhutanese origin. Hence, the Monastery is one of Bhutan's most important Buddhist establishments and a proud jewel of all the community members. It is a symbol of peace, Prosperity, and hope. When the black-necked cranes arrive in Gangtey, they circle above Gangtey Monastery three times before landing on the marsh. They will also repeat the ritual before they depart from Bhutan to Tibet. The architecture and engineering highlight the uniqueness and elegance of traditional Bhutanese architecture, and we recommend experiencing the monastic lifestyle of monks and join the prayer for the most surreal spiritual evening.
