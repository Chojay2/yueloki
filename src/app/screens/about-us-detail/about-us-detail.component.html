About Us

Hotel Yue Lo Ki was established in 2010 as a small guest house with six rooms and five staff. The initial idea was just a small farmhouse next to the old carpet factory to increase their sales of textile goods. Gradually the small guesthouse scaled into a small Hotel with the restructuring of the old carpet factory to a new building with ten rooms.

Hotel Yue Lo Ki has 16 rooms with 11 twin beds and four king-size beds. All the rooms have rich and elegant wooden panels that provide excellent acoustics insulation, great for the cold Phobjikha valley. The walls of windows offer a great view of the valley, with plenty of biodiversity surrounding the hotel. All the rooms have electronic heaters, central water heating systems, and modern shower cabinets to accommodate an excellent shower session after trekking the beautiful trails in the valley.
