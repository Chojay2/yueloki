About Us

We are one of the small hotels in Phobjikha and we aim to provide best services to anyone that comes here. We have been running this business for a decade now and it has been successful till now. We have received travellers both in and outside of Bhutan and everyone has left here with joy. Your experience and well-being is what matter to us and we make sure that you have the best moments and services here.

Our History

This hotel business was found by our family and it has been going on since then. We are one of the oldest people and place in Pobjikha. The knowledge that we have about this place is countless and this is one of the reason why the travellers seek to visit and we are more than happy to share to anyone.
