FACILITY

CHECK-IN / CHECK OUT

The check-in time is from 2:00 pm onwards and upon arrival, luggage racks are available at the reception. Guests must provide their credit card details or make cash payment/bank transfer/EFTPOS payment at the reception desk.

The checkout time is at 10:00 am and we request all guests to settle all accounts in full unless proper credit arrangements have been made. Unfortunately, we do not accept cheques. Please ask the receptionist if you require any additional help during check-in and check-out.

CAR PARK

There is ample complimentary parking space and if you require any directions to the hotel please contact the reception so we can provide directions and ushering services.

ROOM SERVICES

Your rooms will be serviced daily and We respect your privacy, should you not wish to be disturbed, please place the DO NOT DISTURB sign on the outside of your door.

  • WAKEUP CALLS Please contact reception staff if you require a wake-up call.
  • INTERNAL HEATING Rooms will be heated upon your arrival. Some rooms have electronic panel heaters whilst others have traditional burners. Please contact reception if you require any assistance.
  • HAIRDRYERS are located in the drawers for your convenience
  • LAUNDRY FACILITIES are available from 9:00 am to 3:00pm
  • BATHROBES are provided upon request

RESTAURANT

The breakfast buffet opens from 6:00 am to 9:30 am. Dinner buffet services are from 6:00 pm to 9:30 pm, this includes room service.

RECEPTION HOURS

For your convenience our front desk is open24/7. If you require any assistance, please feel free to speak with our friendly reception staff, who will be happy to assist you at any time. Reception can be contacted from your room by calling or messaging the Hotels Whatsapp I.D.

Please note that foreign currency exchange, hire of bicycles, tour booking and administration services are available only from 7:00 am to 9:00 pm. Bus and Taxi services are available but please notify us at the earliest of your convenience.

INTERNET SERVICES

All guests can avail internet with proper speed. Upon arrival, you will be issued a card with the WIFI password.
